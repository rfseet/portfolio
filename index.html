About

A footwear native, built on both sides of the business.

I've spent 4 years in the sports and outdoor industry, and I think of myself as a footwear person first. I'm a competitive trail runner (ITRA Performance Index: 661) who got curious about how shoes are actually built, and that curiosity is what pulled me into the business side of the industry.

I've worked both ends of the value chain. At VF Corporation I sat on the supply side, leading ERP rollouts, creating PLM system roadmaps, and developing systems, processes and dashboards that sourcing and manufacturing teams use every day. At adidas I moved to the demand side, owning category management and go-to-market planning for Running, Outdoor, and Specialist Sports in SEA.

My footwear grounding comes from two places: a Footwear Design & Development Certificate from Lasell University, and years of mentorship from footwear development leaders and product experts at VF, who integrated me into their teams and took the time to teach me how a shoe actually comes together.
