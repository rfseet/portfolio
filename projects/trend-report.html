Trend Report — Authentic Gorpcore / Sportstyle

Role
Midterm Project (Solo)
Duration
1 Week
Trend
Authentic Gorpcore / Sportstyle
Method
Cartogram & Trend Forecasting

Project Overview

Midterm project for a Footwear Materials, Color & Trend Analysis course at Lasell University. The objective was to develop a full trend report in one week. I selected Gorpcore as my trend for analysis.

Skills Acquired

I developed an understanding of the different lenses and techniques used in trend analysis. Using these techniques, I researched industry trends via forecasting services like WGSN to identify short-term microtrends and long-term macrotrends, gaining insight into the footwear product ecosystem and how marketing, product line management, and design shape the work footwear developers do.

Cartogram. Mapping trend innovators, drivers, impact, consequences, and futures for Gorpcore/Sportstyle.
Influencers of Forecasting. Discourse method and personal interest behind the forecast.
Short-Term Microtrend. Sustainable Outdoor Heritage: circularity, biofabrication, and new technology in outdoor footwear.
Power of Three. Cross-industry signal of technicality in nature, from performance running gear to concept vehicles and furniture.
Long-Term Macrotrend. Function x Form: technicality, refinement, and personalization shaping footwear beyond 2025.
